My top 10 movies of all time

  • Dangal

    • Dangal is an inspiring and motivational movie that tells the true story of determination, hard work, and success.

  • Tare Zameen Par

    • Tare Zameen Par is a heartwarming and emotional movie that beautifully portrays the struggles of a child with dyslexia.

  • Sholay

    • Sholay is a timeless action-adventure movie that combines friendship, courage, humor, and sacrifice.

  • Gangs of Wassaypur

    • Gangs of Wasseypur is a gripping crime drama that explores power, revenge, family rivalry, and politics across several generations.

  • Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

    • Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara is a heartwarming and inspiring movie about friendship, self-discovery, and living life to the fullest.
